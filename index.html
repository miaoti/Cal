    Anything else you have tried out

    Yes, apart from the one-key and three-key interfaces, I also experimented with a two-key interface. In this setup, one key was used for "next" and "select" (short press for "next", long press for "previous") and the second key was used for "select". The main goal was to find a balance between simplicity and efficiency, as the one-key interface might be too slow and error-prone due to the long press action, and the three-key interface could potentially be a bit overwhelming for some users.
